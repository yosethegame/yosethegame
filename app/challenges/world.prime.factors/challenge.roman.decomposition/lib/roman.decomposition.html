Imagine a time traveller shows up, arriving directly from the mythical ancient Rome and imagine he'd like to decompose a number using your wonderful app! Yes those things can happen!
Guess what? he uses roman numbers of course!

For example, receiving

http://your-server/primeFactors?number=CCC

You should respond

{
    "number" : "CCC",
    "decomposition" : [ "II", "II", "III", "V", "V" ]
}

Your server will be called with a random number between 1 and 399.

You can find several examples of the Roman kata on the Internet.
